## Incident Overview
A ransomware attack affecting both on-premise systems and cloud-connected resources, resulting in encryption of data and disruption of business operations.

## Initial Detection
- Endpoint security alerts indicating ransomware behavior
- User reports of inaccessible or encrypted files
- Unusual file modification activity across systems
- Alerts from backup or storage systems indicating abnormal behavior

## Investigation & Analysis
- Identify the initial infection vector
- Determine the scope of affected systems and data
- Analyze ransomware behavior and propagation methods
- Assess impact on cloud workloads and connected services

## Containment Actions
- Isolate infected systems immediately
- Disable compromised accounts and credentials
- Restrict network connectivity to limit further spread
- Suspend affected cloud resources if required

## Eradication Steps
- Remove ransomware payloads and persistence mechanisms
- Patch exploited vulnerabilities
- Reset credentials and review access controls

## Recovery
- Restore systems from clean backups
- Validate system integrity and functionality
- Resume business operations in a controlled manner

## Lessons Learned
- Identify gaps in detection and response
- Improve backup, recovery, and resilience strategies
- Update security controls and procedures
